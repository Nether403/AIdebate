# Product-Era Archive

Archived on 2026-05-11 during the revival cleanup.

This folder contains product-era surfaces that conflict with the current research-workbench roadmap but may be useful as historical reference.

## Archived areas

- `app/`: dashboard, leaderboard, statistics, and betting test pages.
- `api/`: prediction, leaderboard, statistics, debate vote, featured debates, and share endpoints.
- `components/`: prediction-market, leaderboard, statistics, and social share UI components.
- `lib/`: prediction-market, rating/leaderboard, and user-vote abuse detection helpers.

## Why archived

The revival roadmap explicitly excludes prediction markets, DebatePoints, Superforecaster badges, personal betting dashboards, public social-sharing mechanics, and consumer-growth features from the MVP.

## Restore guidance

Do not restore these files into active app paths unless the roadmap is explicitly changed. If restored, update tests, schema, docs, and verification expectations first.
